The company's history dates back to 2020 as a medical startup. Today IMM Group is one of the leading health and wellness companies, specializing in medical and aesthetic solutions.

Headquartered in Texas, USA, with branches in South Korea, Turkey and Russia, IMM Group combines state-of-the-art R&D in the United States with world-class manufacturing in Incheon, South Korea. Our mission is to provide safe, effective, and innovative products that enhance both physical well-being and aesthetic appeal.

Company development timeline

2020

The company's history dates back to 2020 as a medical startup. An Americangroup of investors invested money in the development of new medical drugs.

2021

In 2021, it was decided to develop a new botulinum toxin preparation and a drug for lipolysis.

2021-2023

R&D and clinical trials of drugs were carried out for 3 years (from 2021 to 2023).

2023

Upon completion of development in 2023, a manufacturing plant was selected in South Korea using the developed technology.

2024

The launch of finished products took place in 2024.
